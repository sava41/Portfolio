Handsome Squidward

This project started out as a facial sculpting study. I chose a character with well defined facial landmarks as they're beginer friendly compared to a soft/featureless face. All scultping, modeling, and texturing was done in Blender. I decided to make it into a Snapchat lens to test out the new facial blendshapes functionality.

The lens went viral on Tiktok and has since recieved over 100 million views.
