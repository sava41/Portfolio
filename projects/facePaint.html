Face Paint / Alpha Beauty Booth

This was a collaborative project between me and the talented Ines Alpha. I programmed a system in Lens Studio (Snapchats filter development studio) that allows a user to paint with 3d models on their own face. I created my own lens and Ines took that template and remade it with her own style.
