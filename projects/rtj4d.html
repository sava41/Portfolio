RTJ4D Contest

The RTJ4D contest was created by the hiphop duo, Run The Jewels and Sketchfab. The goal of the project was to take the original 3D models from the RTJ4 album cover, and remix them. I knew from the beggining I wanted to do a diorama type scene. Something that has to be viewed in 3d and cannot all be shown in a single picture. The angularity of the original hands made me think of crystals. Then I got the idea to do a dragon guarding his loot. Due to the deadline, I had to scale back the scope of the project to only include the dragons head. Everything was sculpted and modeled in Blender, baked in Marmoset, and texture in Substance Painter. I didn't win, but I'm still proud how this one came out.
