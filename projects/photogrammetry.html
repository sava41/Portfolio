I have been using photogrammetry for over 7 years. Texture extraction and millimeter to sub-millimeter tolerances mean photogrammetry is useful for art asset pipelines and engineering applications.

Monkey Piggy Bank

Carbon Fiber Tub

This scan was done for the uOttawa supermileage team. The carbon fiber chassis was handbuilt. An accurate model was needed to avoid interference issues when designing the interior assemblies. I wasn't allowed to paint or coat the chassis in anything to make it lighter which made the photo dynamic range very low. I ended up using hundreds of colored stickers to get better keypoint detection.

The raw scan data was then processed in 3ds Max and then imported into SolidWorks. The mounting point datums were used to calibrate the geometry size.
